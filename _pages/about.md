Welcome to my webpage!

I am a junior researcher and operational scientific manager at the [Co-innovation lab](https://ecoledesponts.fr/co-innovation-lab) of Ecole Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées, in partnership with Renault Group Supply Chain. Concurrently, I serve as COO of [Praedon](https://www.praedon.com/), where we develop machine learning and operations research solutions for industrial decision-making under uncertainty.

My background includes a PhD from [CERMICS](https://cermics-lab.enpc.fr/) at Ecole Nationale des Ponts et Chaussées, supervised by [Axel Parmentier](https://cermics.enpc.fr/~parmenta/), where I specialized in large-scale inventory routing problems within an industry-academia collaboration with Renault Group.

Research Topics

* Combinatorial optimization
* Contextual stochastic optimization
* Structured prediction
